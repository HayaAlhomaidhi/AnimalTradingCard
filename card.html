Horse

Horses can sleep both lying down and standing up.

  • Scientific Name: Equus ferus caballus
  • Diet: Herbivore (vegetarian)
  • High: 64inches, 163cm
  • Weigh: 700 to 1000 kilograms

Horses are four legged animals that have had a long relationship with humans. They were once a major form of transportation for humans. One more thing about horses, there anatomy enables them to make use of speed to escape predators.
